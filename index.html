Hi there, and welcome! I'm

Muhammad Asjad.

I am an aspiring game programmer hailing from Ontario, Canada, currently studying Game Programming at St. Lawrence College. Growing up in a family of computer programmers, I've always been drawn to the world of technology. However, my motivation to become a game programmer is from my desire to make creative and immersive gaming experiences, particularly in the realms of creative games, horror, and narrative-driven adventures.

I've already dipped my toes into game development, creating projects like my own version of Frogger in SDL and a captivating mini text-based RPG. I excel at project management, clear communication, and am adept at breaking down complex tasks into manageable steps. My skills extend beyond code to pixel art and video editing, allowing me to enhance both the technical and visual aspects of my projects. Ultimately, my goal is to create games that resonate with players, leaving a lasting impression and transporting them to new worlds filled with excitement and wonder.

Welcome to my portfolio! Before you proceed further, allow me to ask: Have you ever played a game that's left a lasting impact on you? Feel free to contact me to let me know!
